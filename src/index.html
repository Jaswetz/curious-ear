Less Strangers. More Friends.

A project that creates spaces for people to connect … who may never otherwise meet. So you can share more, listen more, and learn more. Maybe even make friends out of strangers.

How it works

Through physical installations—a giant ear and a giant mouth—what’s shared in one part of the city can be listened to somewhere else.
